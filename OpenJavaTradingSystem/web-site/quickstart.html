The fast lane
Download the binary distribution of the OpenJavaTradingSystem and unpack it. Then simply follow these steps: 

tar -xzvf OpenJavaTradingSystem-bin-0.10.tar.gz
cd OpenJavaTradingSystem-bin-0.10
chmod u+x sisc.sh
./sisc.sh
(current-directory (getenv "sisc.home")) (load "sisc/functionality.scm") (ojts:init)
(ojts:read-xml-configuration "testread.xml")
(ojts:get-data-for-equity "yahoo-csv" "2004-06-01" "2004-07-01" "DE0007500001" "XETRA" *TRADING-DAY-VOLUME*)
(ojts:display-chart
 (ojts:create-candlestick-chart-for-equity "yahoo-csv" "2004-06-01" "2004-07-01" "DE0007500001" "XETRA"))
(exit)
